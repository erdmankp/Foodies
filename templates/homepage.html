Grocery Proximity

Diabetes Correlations
Calculate the correlation coefficient of diabetes and the other chosen variable. Correlation coefficient result of -1.0 shows a perfect negative correlation between diabetes and the chosen variable and 1.0 shows a perfect positive correlation.
